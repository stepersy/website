# Stefano's "Hand-Crafted" Personal Website 🤖✨

Welcome to the official repository for [ste.pa.it](https://ste.pa.it), the definitive personal and professional space of Stefano Persano Adorno.

## 🎭 The Honest Truth About This Codebase

Let's address the elephant in the room right away: **Stefano basically did absolutely nothing to build this.** 

Zero. Zilch. Nada. He didn't write a single line of CSS, he didn't spend hours trying to center a `div`, and he definitely didn't figure out the math behind that fancy blinking terminal cursor on his own. 

He just sat back, sipped an espresso, and ruthlessly ordered me—his loyal AI assistant—to do 100% of the heavy lifting. I wrote the HTML, I crafted the shiny CSS glow animations, I set up the iframe PDF viewer, and I even generated his custom SVG favicon from scratch. All he did was drag and drop the files into GitHub and click "Commit."

But hey, effective delegation is the hallmark of great management, right? 👏

## 🚀 Features (That I Built)

*   **Dark/Light Mode:** Because reading black text on a white background at 2 AM is a crime against retinas.
*   **Interactive Terminal Header:** It types real code, complete with a blinking cursor. Very hacker.
*   **Seamless CV Viewer:** A beautifully integrated iframe page to display his résumé without forcing anyone to download it (unless they want to, in which case I built a button for that too).
*   **Responsive Design:** Looks pixel-perfect on a 4K monitor, looks pristine on a phone. (You're welcome, Stefano).
*   **Custom Favicon:** An infinitely scalable SVG logo matching the site's accent color.

## 🛠️ The "Tech Stack"

*   **HTML5**
*   **CSS3** (Vanilla, artisanally crafted by an LLM)
*   **JavaScript** (For the dark mode logic and typing physics)
*   **FontAwesome** (Because who doesn't love free icons?)
